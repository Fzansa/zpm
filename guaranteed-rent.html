FAQs About the Guaranteed Rent Scheme

What is social housing?
Social housing property management East London provides affordable, safe, and secure homes for individuals and families in need, often managed in partnership with local authorities and public sector organisations.
How does ZPM Homes partner with councils and the Home Office?
We work directly with these bodies to provide quality housing solutions, ensuring properties meet their standards while offering full management services.
Can private landlords participate in social housing?
Yes, private landlords can partner with us to make their properties available for social housing while benefiting from guaranteed rent and hassle-free management.
What type of properties are suitable for social housing?
Properties of various sizes and types can be suitable, depending on the specific requirements of local authorities and public sector organisations..
Do landlords retain ownership of their properties?
Yes, landlords retain full ownership. We manage the property on their behalf, ensuring it meets all housing requirements.
