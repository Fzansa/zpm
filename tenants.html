FAQs About Tenants

How can I apply for a property?
Submit an application form along with the required documents, such as identification and proof of income. Our team will support you throughout the process.
What types of homes are available?
We manage a variety of properties, including houses and flats, to suit different needs and preferences.
How do I report a maintenance issue?
You can contact our team by phone or email, and we will arrange for the issue to be addressed as quickly as possible.
Is it possible to renew my tenancy?
Yes, tenancy renewals may be offered subject to mutual agreement and property availability.
Are there any additional charges?
There are no hidden costs. Any applicable charges will be clearly explained within your tenancy agreement.
