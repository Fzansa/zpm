About Us

At Zahra Properties Management, we are committed to delivering professional social housing property management services across East London, alongside forward-thinking housing solutions that support both Home Office temporary accommodation landlords and the wider community. Based in London, we specialise in guaranteed rent solutions for East London landlords and work closely with local authorities and public sector organisations to meet housing demands with integrity, reliability, and high standards.

What we do

Zahra Properties Management is a trusted partner in social housing property management across East London, delivering:

GUARANTEED RENT

Landlords can benefit from guaranteed rental income in East London for periods of up to five years. We provide reliable monthly payments and ensure properties are returned in excellent condition, fully maintained and professionally managed.

PROPERTY MANAGEMENT

There are no hidden costs or unexpected charges. The agreed rent is exactly what you receive. We manage your property at no extra cost and handle routine maintenance quickly and efficiently.

SOCIAL HOUSING

We work in close partnership with local councils and public sector organisations to deliver safe, high-quality housing. From tenant placement to complete East London property management, we offer guaranteed rent with a hassle-free process.
