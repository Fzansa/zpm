FAQs for Landlords

How can landlords benefit from partnering with us?
Landlords receive guaranteed rental income, professional property management, and complete peace of mind without dealing with day-to-day tenant issues or maintenance coordination.
Is the rent guaranteed even if the property is vacant?
Yes, landlords receive guaranteed rent for the agreed contract period, regardless of occupancy, ensuring stable and predictable income.
Who is responsible for property maintenance and repairs?
We handle routine maintenance, inspections, and minor repairs. Any major structural work is discussed transparently with the landlord before proceeding.
Do landlords keep full ownership of their property?
Yes, landlords retain 100% ownership of their property. We act only as a managing partner during the contract term.
What types of properties are accepted from landlords?
We accept a wide range of properties including apartments, houses, and HMOs, subject to location, condition, and compliance with local authority standards.
