FAQs for Landlords

What are the benefits of working with us?
Landlords benefit from guaranteed rental income, full property management, and complete peace of mind without handling daily tenant or maintenance matters.
Is rent paid if the property is unoccupied?
Yes, rental payments remain guaranteed for the full contract period, regardless of occupancy, providing consistent and predictable income.
Who manages repairs and ongoing maintenance?
We take care of routine maintenance, inspections, and minor repairs. Any major works are discussed openly with the landlord before moving forward.
Do landlords remain the legal owners?
Yes, landlords retain full ownership of their property at all times. We act solely as the managing partner during the agreement term.
What types of properties do you accept?
We consider houses, flats, and HMOs, subject to location, condition, and compliance with local authority requirements.
